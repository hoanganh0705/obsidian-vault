- Trong trường hợp bí ý/không biết mô tả số liệu thế nào, các bạn cũng tham khảo một số cấu trúc câu sau:

> **The number/proportion/amount of + N** started at **[data]**, after which it experienced a **rise/growth/decline** to **[data]** in **[year]**, before ending the period at **[data]**.

**Ví dụ:**

The number of visitors <span class="hl-danger">staying on cruise ships</span> started at <span class="hl-danger">around 0.25 million</span>, after which it experienced a slight rise to <span class="hl-danger">0.5 million in 2013</span>, before ending the period at <span class="hl-danger">2 million</span>.

---

> ..., **with a subsequent [trend]**, and **a final [trend]**.

**Ví dụ:**

<span class="hl-danger">Roughly 0.75 million visitors stayed on the island</span>, with a subsequent <span class="hl-danger">rise to 1.5 million by 2013</span>, and a final <span class="hl-danger">stagnation at 1.5 million till the end of the surveyed period</span>.

---

> **The figure for + N** was at **[data]**, followed by a **slow/significant increase/decrease** to **[data]**.

**Ví dụ:**

The figure for <span class="hl-danger">total</span> was at <span class="hl-danger">1 million</span>, followed by <span class="hl-danger">a significant increase to 3.5 million by the end of the period</span>.

---

> **The figure for + N + V, and/but + V**

**Ví dụ:**

The figure for <span class="hl-danger">staying on the island increased two-fold from around 0.75 million at the start of the period to 1.5 million in 2013</span>, but later <span class="hl-danger">stagnated at this figure until 2017</span>.
