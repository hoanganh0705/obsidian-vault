- Tiêu chí này đánh giá về khả năng sử dụng từ vựng một cách phong phú, chính xác và linh hoạt trong bài viết của thí sinh. 

- Nhìn chung, tiêu chí này được đánh giá dựa trên các yếu tố: 

- **Range of vocabulary (within the scope of the task)**

(Độ rộng của vốn từ [trong phạm vi đề bài])

- **Word choice + Awareness of style and collocation**

(Độ chính xác trong cách dùng từ+Ý thức về văn phong và kết hợp từ)

(Viết đúng chính tả/ cấu tạo từ)

# 📈 Sample Task 1 Analysis

> The chart shows <span class="hl-success">the time spent by UK residents on different types of telephone calls</span> between 1995 and 2002.

Local fixed line calls were <span class="hl-success">the highest</span> throughout the period, <span class="hl-success">rising</span> from 72 billion minutes in 1995 to just under 90 billion in 1998. After <span class="hl-success">peaking</span> at 90 billion the figure <span class="hl-warning">had fallen back to</span> the 1995 figure by 2002.

National and international fixed line calls <span class="hl-success">grew steadily</span> from 38 billion to 61 billion at the end of the period, although <span class="hl-success">the growth slowed</span> over the last two years.

<span class="hl-success">There was a dramatic increase</span> in mobile calls from 2 billion to 46 billion minutes. <span class="hl-success">This rise was particularly noticeable</span> between 1999 and 2002, during which time the use of mobile phones <span class="hl-success">tripled</span>.

To sum up, although local fixed line calls were still <span class="hl-success">the most popular</span> in 2002, <span class="hl-success">the gap between the three categories had narrowed considerably</span> over the second half of the period.
