# 📈 Sample Task 1 Analysis

> The line graph shows how many <span class="hl-success">stores ceased trading and opened</span> in a particular nation from 2011 to 2018.

---

### 🟦 Overview

<span class="hl-secondary">Overall, the number of closures and openings</span> experienced a downward trend during the period with significant fluctuations resulting in the lead changing several times.

---

### 🟦 Body Paragraph 1 (2011–2014)

<span class="hl-secondary">Commencing with the initial four years</span>, <span class="hl-warning">in 2011</span> openings were <span class="hl-success">higher than closes</span> with approximately 8,500, <span class="hl-success">whilst</span> closures stood at around 6,500.

<span class="hl-warning">Afterwards</span>, both declined and <span class="hl-warning">by 2012</span>, openings had <span class="hl-danger">dropped below closures</span> to just below 4,000, <span class="hl-warning">whereas</span> closures sat at 6,000.

Closures <span class="hl-success">subsequently</span> rose to peak at slightly over 7,000 before falling back to about 6,500 in 2014, <span class="hl-success">whilst</span> openings had a steady increase to reach slightly above 6,000.

---

### 🟦 Body Paragraph 2 (2015–2018)

<span class="hl-secondary">Turning to the remaining period</span>, <span class="hl-warning">by 2015</span> closures had fallen dramatically to about 7,500, <span class="hl-warning">putting it well below openings</span>, <span class="hl-success">whilst</span> openings had a moderate decline to 4,000.

<span class="hl-warning">Although the latter</span> remained at that figure <span class="hl-warning">in 2016</span>, closures bounced back reaching approximately 5,000, once again <span class="hl-success">overtaking openings</span>.

<span class="hl-success">Openings then</span> rose slightly <span class="hl-warning">in 2017</span>, <span class="hl-warning">after which</span> it dropped to 3,000 <span class="hl-warning">by the end of the period</span>.

<span class="hl-secondary">In contrast</span>, closures had a slight drop in 2017 <span class="hl-warning">but</span> ended up back to just above 5,000 in 2018.

# 🚫 Avoid Repetition

<div class="warning-box">

Nếu không dùng **referencing** và **substitution**, bài viết sẽ rất dễ bị lặp từ và cấu trúc.

Ví dụ:

> employees working in transportation... employees working in manufacturing...

Thay bằng:

> Salaries of employees working in transportation and manufacturing...

hoặc

> finance employees...

Bài viết sẽ tự nhiên và đạt điểm **Lexical Resource** cao hơn.

</div>
