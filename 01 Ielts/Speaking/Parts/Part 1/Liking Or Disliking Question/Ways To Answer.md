- Để nói về tổng quát, mặt chung chung, ta có thể dùng các cụm từ sau: 

1. Well, in general I would say … 
2. Generally speaking… 
3. Overall, I think that…

- Để nói về sự cụ thể ta có thể dùng các cụm từ sau

1. To be more specific/ Specifically 
2. In particular
3. To be more precise 
4. Especially/ particularly 

Lưu ý: especially/ particularly thường đứng giữa câu

- Nói về lý do: 

1. And I guess this is probably because… 
2. I think this is due to the fact that… 
3. The reason for this is because… 

VD: 

**Are there any colours you dislike?** 

I absolutely can't stand any shades of pink. Specifically, neon pink really gets under my skin. I think it's because it's just too in-your-face and a bit of an eyesore 

- **In your face:** quá nổ bật (màu)
- **Eyesore:** xấu đến nhức mắt  
- **Get under one’s skin:** Làm ai khó chịu 

**Do you like reading?** 

Well, in general I would say I’m an avid reader. In particular, I do have a thing for thrillers. The reason for this is because this genre gives me the adrenaline I yearn for 

- **Adrenaline:** Sự hưng phấn 

**What’s your favorite food?** 

Well, generally speaking, I’m a big fan of/ I’m partial to Italian food, especially spaghetti. I think this is because I love the creamy texture of their dishes, and the sauce is always top-notch. 

Well, I’d have to say that I’m a big fan of seafood. In particular, I really like to eat Japanese seafood because it’s always so fresh. For instance, sashimi is delicious because the raw fish is so flavorsome.
