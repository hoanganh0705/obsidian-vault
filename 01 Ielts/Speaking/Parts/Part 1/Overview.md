| Technique | Description | Example |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| **DIVISION** | Chia câu trả lời ra làm 2–3 phần nhỏ. | **Q:** What kinds of restaurants are popular in your country?<br><br>**A:** Well, there's definitely a variety of them. I think the most prevalent ones are fast food chains like McDonald's, KFC, or Lotteria. They offer convenience and flexibility, which are undeniably valuable in today's hectic world. Aside from that, in Vietnam, street food stalls hold a special place in the hearts of locals; they're a part of our living culture. You can easily spot these stalls on the pavement, often packed with customers. |
| **GENERAL TO SPECIFIC** | Đi từ tổng quát đến cụ thể. | **Q:** What kinds of food do you like?<br><br>**A:** Well, I gotta say that I'm definitely a foodie, and I do find myself partial to Italian food. More specifically, I'm a big fan of spaghetti. The thing about this dish is that it has such a creamy, juicy tomato sauce, which is top-notch and can definitely wake up your taste buds right away. |
| **DEPENDS** | Đưa ra 2 tình huống. | **Q:** What do you usually do on the weekend?<br><br>**A:** Well, it is contingent upon how I feel. If I'm exhausted after working all week, I prefer to do something relaxing like chilling out on the sofa with a good book. But, if I feel energetic, I would rather do something more exciting like go hiking or camping. |
| **CONCESSION** | Đưa ra phản biện cho câu trả lời chính của bạn. | **Q:** What games did you enjoy playing when you were a child?<br><br>**A:** Well, I was always very keen on playing video games. I spent almost all my free time playing them. However, I'm quite aware that... It wasn't the healthiest way to spend my childhood and perhaps it's contributed to my myopia. |
| **AVOIDANCE** | Lảng tránh trả lời trực tiếp. | **Q:** Where would you like to go for your next vacation?<br><br>**A:** Well, to be honest, I don't really have a clear plan but I guess that I will probably go overseas to some exotic locations like Nepal because I'm really into hiking and exploring new areas. |`
