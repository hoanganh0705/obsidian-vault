# Grammatical Range & Accuracy

> Đánh giá phạm vi và độ chính xác của ngữ pháp.

| Band | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| **9** | - Structures are consistently accurate.<br>- Errors resemble those occasionally made by native speakers. |
| **8** | - Wide range of structures.<br>- Most sentences are error-free.<br>- Only occasional minor mistakes. |
| **7** | - Uses a variety of structures.<br>- Error-free sentences are frequent.<br>- Some basic errors remain. |
| **6** | - Mix of simple and complex structures.<br>- Limited flexibility.<br>- Errors rarely impede communication. |
| **5** | - Basic sentence forms are generally accurate.<br>- Complex structures are limited.<br>- Errors may require reformulation. |

---

## Improvement Path

### Band 5 → 6
- Master simple grammar.
- Begin using complex sentences.

### Band 6 → 7
- Produce more error-free complex sentences.

### Band 7 → 8
- Increase sentence variety.
- Reduce grammar mistakes.

### Band 8 → 9
- Native-like grammar accuracy.
