# Lexical Resource

> Đánh giá vốn từ vựng và khả năng sử dụng từ.

| Band | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| **9** | - Total flexibility and precise use in all contexts.<br>- Sustained use of accurate and idiomatic language. |
| **8** | - Wide vocabulary used flexibly.<br>- Skillful use of less common words and idioms.<br>- Effective paraphrasing. |
| **7** | - Flexible vocabulary for a variety of topics.<br>- Attempts less common vocabulary.<br>- Effective paraphrasing despite some inaccuracies. |
| **6** | - Vocabulary sufficient for discussing topics.<br>- Meaning generally remains clear.<br>- Usually able to paraphrase successfully. |
| **5** | - Vocabulary sufficient for familiar and unfamiliar topics but limited.<br>- Attempts paraphrasing but not always successfully. |

---

## Improvement Path

### Band 5 → 6
- Learn topic vocabulary.
- Practice paraphrasing.

### Band 6 → 7
- Use collocations.
- Use less common vocabulary.

### Band 7 → 8
- Improve idiomatic expressions.
- Use vocabulary more precisely.

### Band 8 → 9
- Native-like flexibility.
- Accurate vocabulary in every context.
