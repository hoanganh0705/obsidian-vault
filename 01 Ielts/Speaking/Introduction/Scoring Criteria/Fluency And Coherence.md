# Fluency & Coherence

> Đây là tiêu chí đánh giá khả năng nói trôi chảy, mạch lạc và phát triển ý tưởng.

| Band | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| **9** | - Fluent with only very occasional repetition or self-correction.<br>- Any hesitation is only used to plan ideas, not to search for vocabulary or grammar.<br>- Speech is naturally cohesive.<br>- Topic development is fully coherent and appropriately extended. |
| **8** | - Fluent with only very occasional repetition or self-correction.<br>- Hesitation may occur but is mostly content-related.<br>- Topic development is coherent, appropriate and relevant. |
| **7** | - Able to keep going and produce long turns without noticeable effort.<br>- Some hesitation or repetition may occur.<br>- Flexible use of discourse markers and cohesive devices. |
| **6** | - Able to keep going and willing to produce long turns.<br>- Coherence may sometimes be lost because of hesitation or repetition.<br>- Uses discourse markers but not always appropriately. |
| **5** | - Usually able to keep going but relies on repetition and self-correction.<br>- Hesitations often occur while searching for vocabulary and grammar.<br>- Overuses discourse markers.<br>- Complex speech often reduces fluency. |

---

## Improvement Path

### Band 5 → 6
- Reduce unnecessary pauses.
- Learn to use discourse markers naturally.
- Speak longer without stopping.

### Band 6 → 7
- Speak longer naturally.
- Improve logical organization.
- Reduce repetition and self-correction.

### Band 7 → 8
- Maintain fluency under difficult topics.
- Develop ideas more naturally.

### Band 8 → 9
- Speak almost effortlessly.
- Extend ideas naturally without hesitation.
